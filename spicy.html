About This Shawarma

Spicy shawarma is designed for those who love bold and fiery flavors. It features marinated meat with extra spices and hot sauce, combined with fresh vegetables, wrapped in soft bread. Perfect for spice enthusiasts!

Recipe Ingredients
  • Chicken or beef strips
  • Shawarma or pita bread
  • Hot chili sauce or harissa
  • Garlic sauce
  • Sliced onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Cucumber strips
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Paprika & chili powder
  • Salt & pepper
  • Vegetable oil
Preparation Procedure
  1. Slice meat thinly and marinate with spices, chili powder, and oil.
  2. Let it rest for 2–3 hours to absorb flavors.
  3. Grill or pan-fry meat until fully cooked and slightly charred.
  4. Warm bread slightly.
  5. Spread garlic sauce and chili sauce on bread.
  6. Add meat and fresh vegetables evenly.
  7. Wrap tightly and heat briefly on the pan for extra crisp.
serving

The spicy shawarma delivers a bold, smoky, and fiery taste. It’s tender, juicy, and full of flavor in every bite. Perfect for those who like their meals with a kick!

Serving Suggestion

Serve with potato fries, extra hot sauce, or a cooling yogurt dip to balance the heat.
