About This Shawarma

Veggie shawarma is a delicious vegetarian alternative that combines fresh crisp vegetables with creamy sauces wrapped in soft pita or shawarma bread. It’s perfect for those who love healthy, flavorful meals without meat.

Recipe Ingredients
  • Pita or shawarma bread
  • Hummus or garlic sauce
  • Lettuce or cabbage
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced cucumbers
  • Grilled bell peppers
  • Carrots
  • Olives
  • Feta cheese (optional)
  • Salt & pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice
Preparation Procedure
  1. Wash and slice all vegetables thinly.
  2. Grill or roast peppers lightly.
  3. Mix vegetables with olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  4. Warm pita bread slightly.
  5. Spread hummus or garlic sauce on the bread.
  6. Add the vegetable mixture evenly.
  7. Optional: sprinkle feta cheese on top.
  8. Roll or fold bread tightly and serve immediately.
serving

The veggie shawarma is fresh, colorful, and bursting with flavor. Each bite combines crisp vegetables, creamy sauce, and soft warm bread, making it a healthy yet satisfying wrap.

Serving Suggestion

Serve with a side of fries, grilled corn, or a chilled soft drink for a complete vegetarian meal.
