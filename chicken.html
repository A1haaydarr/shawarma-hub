About This Shawarma

Chicken shawarma is one of the most popular street foods worldwide. It is known for its tender marinated chicken, grilled to perfection, wrapped in soft flatbread with fresh vegetables and flavorful sauce.

Recipe Ingredients
  • Chicken breast strips
  • Pita or shawarma bread
  • Garlic sauce
  • Sliced onions
  • Fresh tomatoes
  • Cabbage or lettuce
  • Paprika
  • Curry powder
  • Salt & seasoning
Preparation Procedure
  1. Wash and cut chicken into thin strips.
  2. Mix spices, salt, oil, and marinade chicken.
  3. Leave for 1–3 hours.
  4. Grill or pan-fry until golden brown.
  5. Warm bread slightly.
  6. Add vegetables, chicken, and sauce.
  7. Wrap tightly and serve.
serving

You get a juicy, flavorful shawarma wrap with a perfect blend of spices, creaminess, and crunch. Best served hot with fries and a chilled drink.
